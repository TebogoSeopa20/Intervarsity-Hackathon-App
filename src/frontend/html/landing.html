Protecting The Community From dangerous Foods.

Imbewu is an AI-powered food safety scanner that identifies harmful ingredients, expired products, and counterfeit snacks. We're creating safer communities by empowering parents and caregivers with instant product safety information.

The Food Safety Crisis Imbewu Solves

Thousands of children consume dangerous snacks daily from informal vendors, leading to preventable illnesses and long-term health issues.

Unregulated Products

Spaza shops and street vendors sell counterfeit, expired, and illegally manufactured snacks with harmful ingredients that endanger children's health.

Harmful Ingredients

Children are exposed to illegal dyes, excess lead, undeclared allergens, and contaminated ingredients that cause poisoning and allergic reactions.

Lack of Awareness

Parents and caregivers have no way to identify dangerous products, while regulators cannot monitor every informal vendor in underserved communities.

Powerful Food Safety Tools

Our platform combines AI technology with community reporting to create a comprehensive food safety network.

AI Product Scanning

Use your phone's camera to instantly identify snacks, check for harmful ingredients, expiration dates, and verify product authenticity.

Safety Alerts

Receive immediate warnings about recalled products, illegal ingredients, and unsafe snacks reported by other community members.

Vendor Ratings

Check safety ratings for local vendors based on community reports and verified safety inspections to make informed purchasing decisions.

Community Reporting

Report unsafe products and vendors to protect others in your community. Collective reporting creates safer environments for all children.
